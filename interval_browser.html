🎸 Guitar Intervals Visualization 🎸

Systematic visualization of all guitar intervals within 5 frets

📊 System Overview

  • 11 interval types from 2nd to 7th (removed unison/octave)
  • 375 total fingerings - ascending intervals only for clarity
  • Simple & Compound versions - 2nd/9th, 3rd/10th, 4th/11th, etc.
  • Up to 5 frets maximum playable span
  • Smart interval labeling - simple (2, 3, 4) vs compound (9, 10, 11)
  • Organized by root string for systematic study
  • Black dots (R): Root note of interval
  • White dots (♭3, 9, etc.): Interval note with context-appropriate symbols
